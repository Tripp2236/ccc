Soup Wagon Relief

Soup Wagon Relief is a mission to love on and aid our neighbors in need and /or the homeless community in our city and the surrounding cities. Every Tuesday we provide hot homemade meals in Akron and minister to our neighbors in need. We also have an open pantry every Tuesday from 2-5pm. Every Thursday the Soup Wagon deliveries meal boxes to families in our community. And finally, we have an out reach every Sunday afternoon in the Barberton area. We continue to serve, taking the necessary precautions, through this pandemic because we love our neighbors and we know the need is great.

If you would like to donate to this ministry you may do so through the link on the right. Your donation will go entirely and directly to supplying home cooked meals, socks and chapstick for our neighbors in need.
